Prices & Sales Policies Each of our litters are planned and produced for one purpose, bringing forth the next generation of Corso's Kennels Excellence. We hold back puppies from every litter. We do not produce litters for the sole purpose of sales. However, because we do not retain all puppies, we make them available to a select clientele. The people we choose to add to our network family have to meet a strict criterion and ownership requirements, proving they are the best home possible.
If you are only looking for a pet/companion, it is still important to realize that well-bred parents produce well-bred puppies. Even if you are not going to compete with your dog, you still want your companion to look like a top winning Cane Corso, have correct functional structure, come from the healthiest stock (having been given proper health testing), are intelligent, devoted, safe and loyal. We start with the best to produce even better.
Our puppies, available for sale, must spend ample time with the mother and litter first. (2 months minimum).
